This instrument is part of the AI Literacy for Educators research content hosted on Coursx, an open platform supporting AI literacy implementation in teacher development contexts. Developed by Shenoy & Saarela (University of Jyväskylä, Finland), grounded in the Technology Acceptance Model (Davis, 1989; Venkatesh & Bala, 2008) and the GenAI-TPD framework for integrating generative AI into teacher professional development (Shenoy & Saarela, Discover Education, 2026).
